**Preview text:** What our patients actually say about the experience

---

Hi {{contact.first_name}},

It's the question everyone asks — so let's be completely honest about it.

**Before treatment:**
We apply a topical numbing cream 30-45 minutes before the procedure. By the time we start, the treatment area is fully numbed.

**During treatment:**
Most patients describe a warm, tingling sensation. The procedure takes 45-60 minutes for a full face. Our doctors check in with you throughout.

**After treatment:**
Your skin will feel similar to sunburn for the first 24-48 hours. We provide a full aftercare kit and you'll have direct access to our team if you need anything during recovery.

**The recovery timeline:**

- **Days 1-2:** Redness and mild swelling
- **Days 3-7:** Skin begins to peel naturally
- **Day 10:** New skin emerges, makeup can be applied
- **Weeks 6-12:** Collagen continues to rebuild — this is when the real transformation happens

Hi {{contact.first_name}},

We get a lot of the same questions from people considering CO2 laser — so here are the answers to the most common ones.

**"How many sessions will I need?"**
Most patients see dramatic results from a single treatment. Some skin concerns (deep acne scarring, for example) may benefit from 2-3 sessions spaced 6-8 weeks apart. Your doctor will advise during your free consultation.

**"Is it safe for my skin type?"**
CO2 laser can be adapted for most skin types. Our doctors are experienced in treating all skin tones, including skin of colour. Your suitability is assessed at consultation.

**"How long do results last?"**
Results are long-lasting because CO2 laser triggers genuine collagen remodelling. With good skincare and sun protection, patients typically enjoy results for several years.

**"What's included in the free consultation?"**
A full skin analysis with one of our doctors, a SkinCeuticals digital skin scan, and a personalised treatment recommendation. No obligation, no pressure.

Full face treatments start from **£795**. We also offer treatments for neck (£1,300 with face), chest, and hands. Finance options are available.
